Sylvia Plath

1932-1963

Sylvia Plath (1932-63) was a prolific poet for the few years that she was active before her untimely death, by her own hand, aged just 30. But what are her greatest poems? A few titles spring to mind, but it’s not easy to reach a consensus on, say, Sylvia Plath’s top five best poems. But we like a challenge here, so we’ve suggested five of Plath’s finest and most famous poems, along with a little bit about each of them. Check them on the menu above!
